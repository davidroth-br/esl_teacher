Welcome!

I am a private English teacher from the United States with more than 6 years experience and CELTA certification. I am also fluent in Brazilian Portuguese, which helps a lot in understanding the needs of all Latin language speakers (Portuguese, Spanish, French, Italian.)

I teach all levels of general English and have experience with all kinds of students, from complete beginners to advanced learners. I love teaching all aspects of English, but my passion is working on pronunciation with my students....

All my classes are online now because of the COVID-19 pandemic, but that doesn't affect the way I try to make them fun and dynamic. If you want to see for yourself, you can take a placement test right here on the site and book a half hour demo class.
